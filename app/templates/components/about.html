About VitalTrack

VitalTrack is a comprehensive health monitoring platform developed as part of an academic project at UNIVERSITY OF ABUJA in COMPUTER SCIENCE department.

Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health through data-driven insights and personalized tracking tools.

This project combines cutting-edge web technologies with healthcare principles to create a user-friendly platform for monitoring various health metrics.

"Health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being, and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity." - World Health Organization
